Why Gas Safety Matters

Working with gas is not something to take lightly. It’s vital that any work is carried out by a Gas Safe registered engineer. Always ask to see their Gas Safe ID card — and if you're unsure, you can verify their registration by calling the Gas Safe Register on 0800 408 5500.
